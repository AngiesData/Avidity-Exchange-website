Frequently asked questions

Is every category currently available?

No. Some pages are placeholders for future collections and will be marked accordingly.

Will Avidity Exchange sell digital and physical products?

Potentially. Product type, delivery method, shipping terms, and return eligibility will be shown on each listing.

How do service requests work?

Submitting an inquiry does not guarantee acceptance. A project begins only after scope, availability, timing, payment, and applicable terms are confirmed.

Where will payments be processed?

Payments will be handled through a secure third-party checkout provider. Avidity Exchange will not ask customers to send payment-card details through an ordinary contact form.
